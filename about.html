About Me

My name is Ofobutu Abiodun Emmanuel, a passionate Data Analyst and Spatial Data Analyst (GIS) with a degree in Remote Sensing and GIS from the Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA).

My expertise lies in transforming raw data into meaningful insights, with a strong focus on spatial analysis, data visualization, and database management.
I have hands-on experience working with industry-leading tools such as ArcGIS, ArcGIS Online, ERDAS IMAGINE, QGIS, MySQL, Excel, Tableau, Power BI, and Python. My analytical skills allow me to interpret spatial and non-spatial data effectively, enabling businesses and organizations to make informed decisions.

With a keen eye for detail and a problem-solving mindset, I excel at interpreting spatial and non-spatial data, helping businesses and organizations make informed, data-driven decisions. My goal is to develop impactful data solutions that enhance efficiency and drive innovation.
