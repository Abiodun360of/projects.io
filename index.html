Lung cancer dataset exploration

This Lung Cancer Data Exploration consists of 23 SQL queries designed to analyze various aspects of lung cancer cases, including prevalence, risk factors, survival rates, and mortality trends. The queries investigate demographics (age, gender, smoking status), environmental exposures (air pollution, occupational hazards), and country-specific statistics (developed vs. developing nations, mortality rates). They also categorize patients into age groups, assess cancer stage distribution, and evaluate the impact of family history on diagnosis. Additionally, smoking habits, pollution exposure, and early detection rates are examined to uncover patterns in lung cancer cases. These insights can aid health policy-making, resource allocation, and public awareness initiatives
